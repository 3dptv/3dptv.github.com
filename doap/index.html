ptv 3D-PTV is Three-Dimensional Particle Tracking Velocimetry. This project is devoted to the so-called "Zurich software", the 3D-PTV software developed by IGP/IfU at ETH Zurich. "Particle tracking velocimetry (PTV) is one of velocimetry methods, i.e a technique to measure velocity of particles". 3D-PTV is the three-dimensional method, measuring velocity and velocity gradients along particle trajectories. all C TCL scientific & commercial MIT License
