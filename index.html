3D-PTV

Welcome to the open source software for 3D-PTV, three dimensional particle tracking velocimetry


About:

"Particle tracking velocimetry (PTV) is one of velocimetry methods, i.e a technique to measure velocity of particles". 3D-PTV is the three-dimensional method, measuring velocity and velocity gradients along particle trajectories.
