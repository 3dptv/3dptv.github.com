Subscription

Subscription is very simple:

  1. Register to Origo (use the link at the top right on this page or here) and create your own account.
  2. After confirmation of your e-mail address please, send your login name to the ptv@ifu.baug.ethz.ch. Please attach your agreement to fullfil the conditions of the MIT-type license as it is provided below and in the attached file. Make sure you mention the Origo username that used in Step 1. We will then add you to the member list of the 3D-PTV software.

You will be able to download the source code via SVN-checkout as described here. We would gratefully appreciate if our users also develop the software further and submit their patches, fixes and developments for the best of all the users. We encourage cooperation and collaboration on various aspects of the software and particle tracking experiments. See the list of projects we've been working on.
